HOW TO PLAY

OBJECTIVE

Score goals by kicking the ball into your opponent's net. First to 10 goals wins. Match lasts 2 minutes — if tied, 1 minute overtime.

THE BALL

Pick up: Walk into the ball to grab it automatically.

Kick: Click (or FIRE) while holding the ball to kick it where you're aiming. No bullets fire while you have the ball.

Super Kick: Press Shift (or DASH) while holding the ball to launch it straight at the enemy goal.

COMBAT

Shoot: Click to fire bullets at your opponent (when not holding the ball).

Dash: Press Shift to dash in your movement direction (when not holding the ball).

Tackle: Dash into the ball carrier to knock the ball loose!

Death: When eliminated, you respawn after 3 seconds and drop the ball.

CONTROLS

WASD Move
CLICK Shoot / Kick ball
SHIFT Dash / Super kick

Use the joystick to move. FIRE button shoots or kicks. DASH button dashes or super kicks.
